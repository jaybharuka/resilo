# Phase 3 Design Partner Program

## Goal
Get at least 1 external team actively using Resilo during Phase 3.

## Target Segments
1. Indian SaaS startup
2. Fintech startup
3. Internal product team pilot

## Offer
- Free pilot access for 6 weeks
- Weekly 30-minute feedback call
- Shared issue tracker and response SLA

## Success Metrics
- Weekly active users
- Real incidents remediated automatically
- MTTR reduction vs baseline
- Retention after trial period
